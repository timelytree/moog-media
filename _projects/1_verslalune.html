Vers la Lune is a Toronto-based womenswear brand. Each of the five pieces in its Spring/Summer 2017 collection is unique and hand made by local designer, Jennifer Jugowiec. With sharp silhouettes and a soft colour palette, it strives for a delicate balance between strength and grace, creating a powerful femininity that mirrors the young women of our generation.

In helping to bring the collection to life, we produced both a product and editorial photoshoot, stitched together its final runway showcase at Toronto's Mass Exodus, and were there behind the scenes, capturing what it took to make it all happen.
